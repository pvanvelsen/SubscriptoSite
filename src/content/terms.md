## 11. Data Synchronization and Offline Mode

### 11.1 Real-Time Sync

Subscriptin uses **Supabase real-time synchronization** to keep your subscription data consistent across all your devices. Sync features include:
- Bidirectional sync (changes on any device propagate to all devices)
- Automatic conflict resolution using "Last-Write-Wins" strategy
- Offline queue for changes made while disconnected

### 11.2 Offline Mode

You can use Subscriptin without an internet connection. When offline:
- All data is stored locally in a SQLite database on your device
- Changes are queued and uploaded when you reconnect
- No data is lost if you remain offline

### 11.3 Sync Limitations

Sync may fail or experience delays due to:
- Poor network connectivity
- Third-party service outages (Supabase)
- Device battery optimization settings (which may pause background sync)
- Conflicting changes made simultaneously on multiple devices

**We are not liable for data loss, corruption, or sync failures.**

### 11.4 Disabling Sync

You may disable sync in the App settings. Disabling sync will:
- Keep your data local to your device only
- Prevent changes from uploading to the server or syncing to other devices
- Not delete data from the server (previously synced data remains)
